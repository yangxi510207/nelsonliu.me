About Me

I'm an undergraduate at the University of Washington, where I'm studying linguistics and statistics.

I work on research as a member of Noah's ARK, and I am advised by Noah Smith. My broad research interests lie in and at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning.

Outside of work I enjoy contributing to open source, particularly to the scikit-learn project. Beyond computer-related things, I like hiking, photography, and attempting to cook.

I'm currently a research intern at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2), where I'm generally working on applying deep learning to reading comprehension tasks.
